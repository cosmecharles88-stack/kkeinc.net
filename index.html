About KKE

A concrete restoration company built for real field conditions.

KKE Concrete Lifting specializes in restoring settled, sunken, and unsupported concrete without unnecessary demolition. Our work is focused on practical results: improved safety, better drainage, stabilized slabs, reduced downtime, and longer concrete service life.

From municipal roadways and bridge approaches to commercial slabs, warehouse floors, and residential concrete, KKE brings field-tested experience, specialized equipment, and a clean project approach to every job.
